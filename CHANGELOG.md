# What's New

## 1.2.1 (2026022602) - 2026-02-26

What's New in Métropolist:

[NEW FEATURES]
• Replay Onboarding: Revisit the guided tour anytime from the Help section in Settings.

[IMPROVEMENTS]
• Error Screen: Fatal errors during app start now show what went wrong instead of silently crashing.
• Error Handling: Profile and line detail screens now show an error message instead of a blank page when data loading fails.
• Celebration Accessibility: VoiceOver now reads XP breakdown, badge unlocks, achievement details, and level progress on the travel success screen. The secret achievement overlay is properly marked as modal.

## 1.2.0 (2026022601) - 2026-02-26

What's New in Métropolist:

[NEW FEATURES]
• Favorite Lines & Stations: Star any line or station from its detail page. Favorites appear as a dedicated section at the top of the Lines and Stations tabs.
• Home Screen Quick Action: Long-press the app icon to start a new travel directly.
• Extended Statistics: Your profile now shows weekly activity charts, busiest day/hour breakdowns, top 5 most visited stations and most used lines, plus department and fare zone coverage with progress bars.
• Preferences: New settings for destination sort order (route vs alphabetical), nearby station radius (200m–2km), and map style (standard/satellite/hybrid).
• Developer Mode: Tap the build number 5 times in Settings to reveal debug info sections on line, station, and travel detail pages.
• New Loading Animation: A custom transit-themed loading indicator replaces the default spinner throughout the app.

[IMPROVEMENTS]
• Faster profile and search loading with reduced redundant work.
• Smoother scrolling in achievements and station lists.
• Better VoiceOver support and reduced-motion compliance across the app.

[BUG FIXES]
• Fixed date display issues on certain regional calendar settings.
• Fixed iCloud status getting stuck on "Checking" when offline.
• Fixed nearby station search not respecting the configured radius.
• Fixed broken translations and formatting issues in French localization.

## 1.1.0 (2026022501) - 2026-02-25

What’s New in Métropolist:

[NEW FEATURES]
• Fresh Onboarding: A brand-new guided experience to help you master recording travels, earning rewards, and exploring the Île-de-France network.
• Station Search: Find any stop across the entire network instantly.
• Reimagined XP & Leveling: Progression has been totally overhauled. Track your growth with a new Level Card on your profile, featuring category breakdowns and progress tracking for streaks, discoveries, and more.
• Secret Achievements: 10 hidden achievements are waiting to be found!
• New Celebration Screen: Every trip is a win! Experience a multi-phase animated summary with cascading XP and haptic feedback.
• FAQ: Have questions? Our new Help section in Settings covers everything from XP earning to data exports.

[IMPROVEMENTS]
• Smarter Labels: We now use transit-specific terms — "Direction" for Metro/Tram, "Mission" for RER, and "Terminus" for Bus.
• Tech Stats: Check the database size and route branch counts directly in Settings.

[BUG FIXES]
• Fixed UI persistence where line groups would collapse after navigating away.
• Improved Noctilien detection using official metadata for better accuracy.
• Enhanced location stability with a manual refresh button and 60-second caching to save battery.
• Corrected badge count logic to properly reflect Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers.
• Fixed layout breaks for long operator names and the "View All" travel history button.
• Improved error handling for denied location permissions.
