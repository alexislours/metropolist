# What's New

## 1.1.0 (2026022301) - 2026-02-23

What’s New in Métropolist:

[NEW FEATURES]
• Fresh Onboarding: A brand-new guided experience to help you master recording travels, earning rewards, and exploring the Île-de-France network.
• Station Search: Find any stop across the entire network instantly.
• Reimagined XP & Leveling: Progression has been totally overhauled. Track your growth with a new Level Card on your profile, featuring category breakdowns and progress tracking for streaks, discoveries, and more.
• Secret Achievements: 10 hidden achievements are waiting to be found!
• New Celebration Screen: Every trip is a win! Experience a multi-phase animated summary with cascading XP and haptic feedback.
• FAQ: Have questions? Our new Help section in Settings covers everything from XP earning to data exports.

[IMPROVEMENTS]
• Smarter Labels: We now use transit-specific terms — "Direction" for Metro/Tram, "Mission" for RER, and "Terminus" for Bus.
• Tech Stats: Check the database size and route branch counts directly in Settings.

[BUG FIXES]
• Fixed UI persistence where line groups would collapse after navigating away.
• Improved Noctilien detection using official metadata for better accuracy.
• Enhanced location stability with a manual refresh button and 60-second caching to save battery.
• Corrected badge count logic to properly reflect Bronze, Silver, and Gold tiers.
• Fixed layout breaks for long operator names and the "View All" travel history button.
• Improved error handling for denied location permissions.
