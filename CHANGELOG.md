# What's New

## 1.0.0 (2026022301) - 2026-02-23

Métropolist is here! Explore, track, and complete every transit station in the Paris region.

- Browse all Ile-de-France transit lines: Metro, RER, Train, Tram, Bus, Cable Car, Funicular, and more
- Log travels and track visited stations across the network
- Gamification: earn XP, level up, collect line badges, and unlock achievements
- Per-line and per-mode completion progress with visual rings
- View line routes on an interactive map
- Sync your progress across devices with iCloud
- Export and import your travel data as JSON
- Search lines by name or number
- Available in English and French
- No tracking, no ads, no data collection — your data stays yours
